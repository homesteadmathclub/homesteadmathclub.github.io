About Math Club

HHS Math Club is a group of students who immensely enjoy problem solving and seek to learn more about math. Members come to the Math Club to enjoy fun activities, such as competitive guts rounds and game nights, and also to learn about advanced topics such as combinatorics, game theory, and number theory. In addition, the officer team organizes guest talks to introduce members to new topics and facilitates registration for competitions like the AMCs, the BMT, and the SMT.
